Description

Theoretical and practical course on iuxta-subperiosteal implants with live surgery. It focuses on resolving severe maxillomandibular atrophy using non-regenerative techniques with subperiosteal implants.

Participants will learn a digitally implemented protocol for bone atrophy management and surgical workflow from design to production.

B&B Dental live surgeries and clinical case observations are included for hands-on understanding.
